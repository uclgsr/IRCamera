Recording pipeline for the thermal IR camera and the GSR sensor suite.

```mermaid
graph TD
    subgraph Thermal_IR_Path
        A[Thermal IR Camera Optics] --> B[Thermal Calibration & NUC]
        B --> C[Frame Grabber & RAW Buffer]
        C --> D[Radiometric Conversion]
        D --> E[Noise Filtering & Dead Pixel Repair]
        E --> F[Motion Compensation & ROI Tracking]
        F --> G[Timestamp + Sync Marker]
    end

    subgraph GSR_Path
        H[GSR Electrodes (Palmar Sites)] --> I[Constant-Current Source]
        I --> J[Differential Amplifier]
        J --> K[Anti-Alias Filter]
        K --> L[24-bit ADC Sampling]
        L --> M[Digital Filtering (HP + LP)]
        M --> N[Artifact & Motion Rejection]
        N --> O[Timestamp + Sync Marker]
    end

    G --> P[Shared Clock Bus]
    O --> P

    P --> Q[Multi-Modal Synchronizer]
    Q --> R[Sensor Fusion Buffer]
    R --> S[Quality Monitor & Alerts]

    S --> T[Real-Time Operator Console]

    R --> U[Feature Extraction Pipeline]
    U --> V[Thermal Metrics (ΔT, perfusion)]
    U --> W[GSR Metrics (EDA tonic/phasic)]
    V --> X[Combined Feature Vector]
    W --> X

    X --> Y[Session Metadata Tagger]
    Y --> Z[Recording Controller]

    Z --> AA[Redundant Data Logger]
    AA --> AB[Local SSD Storage]
    AA --> AC[Encrypted Telemetry Stream]

    AB --> AD[Post-Session Batch Analysis]
    AC --> AE[Remote Analytics Cluster]

    S -.-> AF[Self-Test & Calibration Reports]
    AF -.-> AD
```
